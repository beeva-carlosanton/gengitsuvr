Cookies Policy

The site http://takethejuice.com uses cookies, which are small files that the server sends to a user’s computer to collect information that improves browsing of the site. Cookies are a regular technique at web sites, and they carry out functions that are considered necessary for the correct operation and viewing of a site. These cookies do not permanently record any information on the hard drive of a user’s computer.

The cookies used on this site only collect anonymous information of the user (type of browser, operating system, or IP address). In no event do they provide references that allow a user to be identified with personal data (such as their name and surnames).

Users may freely decide on whether or not said cookies are implemented through a message that is displayed the first time the site is accessed, as well as decide on the elimination of the cookies through the browser help section. Users may also set up their browser to accept or reject all cookies by default or receive an on-screen notice of the reception of every cookie and decide at that time whether or not they should be implemented. However, users must keep in mind that rejecting all cookies or expressly rejecting the cookies of this site will not completely prevent viewing of the page or browsing on the page.
